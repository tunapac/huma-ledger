# Technical Whitepaper for Humanity Coin ($HUMA)

## Abstract
Humanity Coin ($HUMA) aims to create a blockchain ecosystem focused on social impact, leveraging innovative tokenomics and a user-friendly design to empower users and contributors.

## Tokenomics
- **Total Supply**: 1 Billion $HUMA tokens
- **Initial Distribution**:
  - Public Sale: 60%
  - Team & Advisors: 15%
  - Ecosystem Fund: 15%
  - Reserve: 10%

## Soul-Pegged Valuation System
- **Definition**: Soul-pegging connects $HUMA value to verified human contributions, creating a trust layer based on social impact.
- **Mechanism**: Users earn $HUMA by proving their social contributions, which are verified through a decentralized network.

## Smart Contract Architecture
- **Layer 1**: Based on Ethereum-compatible blockchain.
- **Security Features**: Multi-signature wallets, real-time auditing and monitoring.
- **EIP-Standards**: Utilizes ERC-20 and ERC-721 standards for token creation and management.

## $HUMASD Stablecoin Mechanism
### Characteristics:
- **Pegged to USD** with a dynamic supply mechanism.
- **Backing reserve** model that utilizes part of the gains from $HUMA transactions to maintain its value.

## Mining Allocation
- **Mining Schedule**:
  - 5% of tokens will be allocated over 10 years.
  - **Halvings** occur every 3 years to control supply and incentivize early miners.

## Vesting Schedules
- **Team Tokens**: 4-year vesting period with a 1-year cliff.
- **Investor Tokens**: Distributed over 24 months.

## Ecosystem Design
1. **Decentralized Applications (DApps)**: Development of social impact DApps on top of the $HUMA network.
2. **Partnerships**: Collaborations with NGOs and social enterprises to create a broader impact.
3. **Community Engagement**: Regular events and initiatives to educate users about the ecosystem and foster collaboration.

## Conclusion
Humanity Coin ($HUMA) is designed to leverage blockchain technology for social good, creating a comprehensive ecosystem that incentivizes positive contributions to society. With its innovative tokenomics and robust architectural framework, $HUMA is set to usher in a new era of blockchain-driven social change.
