# PROJECT HUMA-LEDGER: LOAN SUMMARY
# Amount Requested: $250,000,000 (Phase 1)

## THE PROBLEM
70% of rural Nigeria lacks secure, affordable digital identity and connectivity, costing the economy billions in fraud and lost productivity.

## THE SOLUTION
A Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network (DePIN) utilizing satellites and modular foundries to provide "Free-to-Air" 8G Alpha connectivity.

## THE REPAYMENT
Repayment is automated via a small service fee on the Huma-Ledger, ensuring the loan is serviced regardless of traditional banking hurdles.
