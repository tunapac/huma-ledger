# ROADMAP for Humanity Coin Blockchain Development

## Development Phases

1. **Research and Planning**  
   - **Timelines:** 2026-03 to 2026-06  
   - **Milestones:**  
     - Research existing blockchain technologies  
     - Gather community feedback  
     - Create initial project specifications

2. **Prototype Development**  
   - **Timelines:** 2026-07 to 2026-09  
   - **Milestones:**  
     - Develop a minimum viable product (MVP)  
     - Initial testing and iteration  

3. **Beta Launch**  
   - **Timelines:** 2026-10 to 2027-01  
   - **Milestones:**  
     - Launch beta version to selected users  
     - Collect feedback for improvements

4. **Mainnet Launch**  
   - **Timelines:** 2027-02  
   - **Milestones:**  
     - Launch mainnet  
     - Marketing and partnership outreach

## Future Development

- Continuous updates based on user feedback  
- Regular audits for security and performance improvements  
- Expanding ecosystem through partnerships and community initiatives

## Conclusion
This roadmap serves as a guide for all stakeholders involved in the Humanity Coin blockchain development. Adjustments may be made as needed to respond to the evolving landscape of technology and user needs.
